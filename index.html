    A SECRET HIDEAWAY
    in the heart of Chester

    The Townhouse & Secret Garden is one of Chester’s hidden gems and one of the best independent hotels in the city. Located within the historic Roman walls which encompass the mediaeval centre. The Townhouse & Secret Garden with its own townhouse Georgian features with Chester’s famous Tudor and Victorian architecture.

    Whether you’re in pursuit of a hideaway for a romantic weekend away, a family getaway or a place to celebrate with friends, a warm welcome awaits!

    STAY, PLAY & CELEBRATE
    Just a few reasons why we think you'll like us...

    No.1. The perfect Chester location

    for a break away with everything in walking distance

    Just moments away from the picturesque River Dee and its beautiful Groves, The Chester Townhouse & Secret Garden is a short stroll from some of the city’s best boutiques, Tudor Rows and attractions.

    find out more about the area

    No.2. Escape to our enchanted Secret Garden

    All year round holiday vibes

    We like to think our garden is just a little bit special offering the very best of the great outdoors whilst ensuring you are always warm & dry too! It’s the heart of our place, an oasis in the city and the focus for our events. It’s also available to hire.

    our garden is your garden

    No.3. Our roman vault & amphitheatre apartment

    space to celebrate

    Not many places can boast a roman vault that sleeps 10 or an amphitheatre apartment that sleeps 8, as well as classic, family and executive rooms?

    It’s what makes us different.

    Do one think well, so the saying goes. Well we focus on the good stuff in life – you know the times that you will remember. Whether that’s cocktails on a balmy evening with friends, Afternoon Tea with your mum or a gathering of family.
